## One-paragraph summary

The disclosed system combines six elements into a single SaaS platform: (1) AI-adaptive natural-language interviews of every employee, scoring answer depth across four dimensions and verifying coverage across sixteen analysis axes; (2) automatic conversion of each interview result into a six-field standardized **Implementation Blueprint**; (3) per-customer multi-tenant dashboards with isolated workspaces, BYOK encryption keys, and a five-level role hierarchy; (4) dynamic deployment of completed automation deliverables as live menu/feature items inside each customer's dashboard upon inspection PASS; (5) continuous measurement of six operational KPIs (`executions_count`, `success_rate`, `avg_processing_time`, `cost_per_execution`, `human_intervention_rate`, `review_ng_rate`) with automated `HEALTHY / WARNING / UNHEALTHY` state labelling; and (6) a **closed-loop promotion mechanism** that automatically re-interviews task owners on a configurable schedule (or upon KPI degradation), recomputes the classification, promotes (or demotes) the automation tier, and reallocates the recovered human capacity to other un-automated tasks ranked by `business_impact × automation_feasibility × (1 − current_automation_coverage)`. The combination — and in particular element (6) — converts a one-shot classifier into a self-improving automation portfolio.

The full disclosure, including variant embodiments (alternative LLMs, alternative dimensionalities, alternative deployment channels, alternative role and pricing models, alternative tier-mapping thresholds), is in [`PUBLIC_DISCLOSURE_EN.md`](./PUBLIC_DISCLOSURE_EN.md).
